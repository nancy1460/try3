Our Story

Nicknamed the "Forest City", located in northern Illinois, halfway between Galena and Chicago you'll find the beautiful city of Rockford. With a population of 147,651 (2016), it has become one of Illinois largest cities.

With its growing downtown area Rockford is becoming a popular place to live/visit.

There is always something to do in Rockford, from the City Market, Dinner on the Dock, Rockford day to Stroll on State in the winter.

Events

There is always something fun going on in Rockford!

City Market:

Takes place every Friday starting May 18 - Sept 28 from 3:30-8:30pm (Closes 1 hour earlier after Labor Day). The Rockford City Market is free admission, live music, and good atmosphere while you shop with the many varierty local vendors from the area.

Rockford Day:

Anually on August 15th, the day is intended to promote the people and the places that make Rockford the great city it is. Invites local businesses to celebrate by offering Rockford Day themed events, specials and discounts.
